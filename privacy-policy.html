Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

At YJ Menswear, we collect basic information when you place an order, such as your Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and Shipping Address. This is only used to process and deliver your orders.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Sending order status updates via Email or WhatsApp.
  • Improving our customer service.

3. Data Security

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) to securely store your information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Order Privacy

Customers can only see their own order history by logging into their secure account. Your personal details are never shown publicly.
